Movie Night Invitation

Simple iOS APP

SwiftUIXcode

Movie Invitation

After clicking the ticket button on the bottom left of the screen, it toggles between invitation accepted and invitation declined.

Pick Your Favorite Movies

After clicking the movie button on the bottom right of the invitation screen, vote for your favorite movies by swiping left or right.

When the user swipes left, they are voting against watching that movie. When the user swipes right, they are voting for watching the movie.
